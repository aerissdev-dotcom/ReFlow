ReFlow was built because most addiction trackers get it wrong. They shame you for slipping, reset your progress, and feel cold and clinical. ReFlow is different – it tracks your clean days with a simple weekly circle system that never resets your overall count. Here are 3 main Quality of Life improvements:

Why It Matters

Addiction is quite... isolating. Existing tools might "punish" you for slipping or require accounts and subscriptions. ReFlow keeps it kind of simple. Your data never leaves your device and there is no signup needed. It clearly improves the quality of life.

What Makes It Different

Unlike most trackers, ReFlow never resets your total clean days when you slip. The weekly circles are honest about this week, but your overall progress stays. The AI companion listens without judgment – no scripted responses, no generic advice. And the Impulse button gives you a moment to breathe when the urge hits hardest.

How It Helps

Visual progress tracking makes your streak tangible. The AI companion provides real-time support without judgment. And the Impulse button interrupts cravings before they become actions. Together, they make recovery feel less like a punishment and more like something worth fighting for.
