ReFlow helps you stay accountable during addiction recovery by tracking your clean days on a simple weekly circle tracker. Each circle fills when you stay clean. When you slip, that day stays empty but your overall count never resets. The app also includes an AI companion for support and daily check-ins to track your mood over time.
